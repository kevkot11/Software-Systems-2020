The Game of Pig

How to play: The game is simple. You roll the dice and you will
land on a number between 1 and 6. You can roll the dice as many
times as you want. You accumulate points that which you can
cash in by ending your turn, which can be seen in the bank.
This is where the kicker comes in. If you roll a 1, the bank is reset to 0
and you lose your turn; thus, getting no points.
First to 100 points wins. Pressing "New Game" Resets all the scores. GLHF.
